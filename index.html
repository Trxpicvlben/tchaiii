    Empowering youth
    Transforming education for a brighter future

    Myrhann Foundation is a dedicated NGO in Côte d’Ivoire, committed to providing educational support to children and youth aged 3 to 25. We focus on promoting education and language skills through engaging activities such as English language initiation, cultural and artistic events, and personal development programs. Our mission empowers the next generation by equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in an ever-changing world.
